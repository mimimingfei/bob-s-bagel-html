Bob's Bagels

Freshly baked, filled and served to you daily.

Baking bagel since 1998

Bob's Bagel, established in 1998, is a quaint bakery specializing in delicious, handcrafted bagels. With over 25 years of experience, Bob's has become a staple in the community for its warm, welcoming atmosphere and its commitment to using only the freshest and highest-quality ingredients. From classic flavors like plain to unique creations like blueberry and cinnamon raisin, there's something for everyone at Bob's. Stop by and treat yourself to a fresh, hot bagel and a cup of coffee, and discover why Bob's has been a beloved destination for locals and visitors alike for over two decades.
